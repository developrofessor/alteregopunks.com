What are the Alter Ego Punks?
- The Alter Ego Punks is a NFT collection on the Avalanche blockchain with two versions. The 1st version has a total of 1k NFTs that will be listed on NFFTrade's marketplace. The 2nd version has a total of 10k NFTs that will be listed on a independent marketplace. The project was created on 12/25/2021.
When is the release date?
- On January 1st, 2022 the project went live to the public.
How can I mint an Alter Ego Punk?
- Click the "MINT" button on the home page and you will be redirected to NFTrade's marketplace where the v1 collection is listed. Adjustments will be made to this website when the v2 collection is ready to launch.
Why should I join this community?
- The Alter Ego Punks entire goal is to bring positivity, utility and benefits to the community. We want to help in any way possible and we are driven to do so by any means necessary!
When AMAs will be held?
- Every Friday a community AMA will be held in the discord server.
